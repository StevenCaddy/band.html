CD List

  • Every Open Eye
    The band's newest album. A further expansion on their Scottish electronic pop style.
  • The Bones of What You Believe
    A combination of Scottish indie-pop and electronic dance music. This was the band's breakthrough album with the smash hit "Mother We Share." Larry Fitzmaurice of Pitchfork.com writes "Chvrches embodies what a generation raised on electronic music is looking for in a rock bank."
  • Recover EP
    Chvrches debut album. Has hit singles like "Lies" and "Recover". More of a demo or teaser than a full album.
